Attention! The project is under active development. There are currently no public instances, and the app itself is not intended for launch standalone, unlike its predecessors. At the moment it is better to use past projects.

An extensible platform for analyzing open data. Essentially, it is the compilation of a dozen of my social network analysis projects. Something like maltego or lampyre, but they’re not invented here and have a different architecture.

Uses WAMP for interacting between application components. This also allows you to write modules on different languages, host them on different hosts, etc
