Just create a new private telegram channel, add that bot as an administrator and send some music to the channel. Tht bot can:

— Replace links to sound attachments (via youtube-dl)
— Convert mp3 to VBR via LAME
— Detect track by its fingerprint (via acoustid)
— Extract title and artist from title string
— Attach cover image
