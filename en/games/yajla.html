A minimalistic application for memorizing kana (syllabic alphabet in Japanese). It automatically adjusts the complexity and frequency of different characters, taking into account all the smallest factors, so instead of thinking about where to start, you can just start!

What is going on in the program behind the scenes can be read in the repository, there is more detailed and technical readme.

By the way, it has a beautiful light theme, but by default it is dark as on this site
